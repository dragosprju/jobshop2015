Intelligent Bee


Intelligent Bee is a web development company proudly based in Iasi, Romania. We provide high-quality web development focused on effective communication and reliability helping companies around the world build great software products and deliver value to their customers.

We are using technologies like Symfony, Ruby/Rails, PHP, HTML, OOP, CSS, Javascript. We also build and maintain Tech Support teams for our customers, formed of engineers who like to help people and solve problems. And we don’t mean “call-center” but really technical, solve-it-now support. One of our happy customers is Sendgrid.com who relies on us for a part of their Support and Development activities. SendGrid is a cloud-based service that replaces a company’s email infrastructure and provides everything it needs to operate a reliable and scalable outbound email delivery system.

Positions that we offer so you can grow your career:
  • Web developer with Symfony skills;
  • Frontend developer;
  • Web designer;
  • QA engineer;
  • Tech Support Engineer.
